Logo Usage Guidelines

  • Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "M" in Meauxbility
  • Never alter the logo proportions or typography
  • Use only approved color combinations
  • Ensure sufficient contrast against background colors
  • Scale proportionally - never stretch or compress
  • Include alternative text for accessibility
  • Use scalable formats (SVG) for digital applications
  • Test visibility on both light and dark backgrounds

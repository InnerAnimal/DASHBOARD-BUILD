Open

InnerAnimal Fund

Recovery Grant Program — Covers therapies, travel, and critical recovery costs

Financial support for SCI survivors to access proven treatments and rehabilitation services. Includes guidance on documentation and timelines.

Meaux Life Fund

Equipment & Technology — Custom wheelchairs, assistive tech, home adaptations

Allocations for essential equipment and adaptive technology that immediately improve independence and safety for daily living.

How to Apply

Clear, transparent steps. Expect honest timelines and real support.

1) Check Eligibility SCI verification, residency, and program fit
2) Prepare Documents Medical note, invoices/quotes, income snapshot
3) Submit Application Online form with details and preferences
4) Review & Follow-ups We may request clarifications or updates
5) Decision Notification with next steps and timeline
6) Disbursement & Support Funds/equipment + community onboarding

Eligibility Checklist

  • ✓Documented SCI (or qualifying diagnosis)
  • ✓Defined need (therapy, equipment, sponsorship)
  • ✓Quote/estimate or care plan attached
  • ✓Willing to share impact updates

Required Documents

  • ✓Physician/therapist letter (as applicable)
  • ✓Invoice/quote for services or equipment
  • ✓Income statement (confidential)
  • ✓Short personal statement (goals & timeline)

Programs FAQ

How long does review take?
Typical window is 2–4 weeks depending on volume and documentation completeness.
Can I apply to multiple programs?
Yes. Indicate priorities in your application — we'll route to the best fit.
Are funds paid to me or the provider?
Usually directly to providers/equipment vendors. Exceptions are explained on award.
Do you support outside the U.S.?
We're building global capacity; some programs are already accessible internationally.
